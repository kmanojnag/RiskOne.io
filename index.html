Welcome to RiskOne.io

RiskOne.io is a unique value proposition to solve a specific problem in cybersecurity around vendor management. RiskOne is the Salesforce of vendor cybersecurity – an online system to enable organizations to evaluate and manage the security posture of the third parties they share YOUR information with.

Pricing

The third parties have a single level of annual subscription while the customers can choose a basic level or a premium level. The premium gives customers more flexibility through customizations and enhanced reporting.

Features
  • Multi-platform accessibility
  • Analytics using Artificial Intelligence
  • Customizable questionnaires
  • Re-usable questionnaires
  • Flexible and customized reporting
  • Facilitated collaboration for vendor and Customers
Non-disclousre agreement

You are not allowed to re-distribute the questionnaires from this portal

About RiskOne.io

Product

RiskOne.io is a multi-platform application that gives our customers the leverage to answer and analyze questionnaires from any device, be it a laptop or a mobile. Our key differentiators can be seen with our Vendor-side features include the automatic and real-time assessment through vendors, ability to leverage consulting advice on the fly, and quick purchase of remediation guidance.

Our Dashboards

With a real-time auditing dashboard, our customers can assess how effective their firm's security policies are being implemented. With the security compliance summary and vendor grades, all sections of a security audit are at our customer’s fingertips, so they can be confident their security management team will identify security compliance issues, and deal with them accordingly.
